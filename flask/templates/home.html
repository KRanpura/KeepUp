✨KEEPUP✨

WELCOME TO OUR MISSION

Did you some species of bamboo can grow almost 3ft a day? If you ever had bamboo in your yard, you know that keeping up maintenance of these fast growing plants can be overwhelming, similar to any current college student keeping up with their educational trends. Whether you are a political science major keeping up with current events or a finance major staying up to date to the stock market and new economical advancements, sometimes it can feel like the news moves quicker then we do. However, we at JPAK propose the perfect solution. Meet KEEPUP, a fresh website that collects data throughout different, reputable sources and merges them right into your profile page. With just a few clicks of a button, you can get up to date information on any education topic that you can think of.

❓HOW DOES IT WORK❓

With our talented group of coders, JPAK had created the vision of a python-backed webpage that makes keeping up to date to currents trends as simple and user friendly as possible. Taking educational interests that the user may have, these interests will be stored into a Syqel Lite database. We use Beautiful Soup to scrape data from websites using the same interests stored as keywords in order to find relevant and recent articles. In the final step, Open AI is used to combine data from the top articles in order create a easy-to-read summary. With this primary goal, a college student can find themselves keeping up to date easier than ever.
